Desserts and Other Treats

Snowballs (Russian Tea Cakes)

Around Christmas my husband starts his annual crave for this cookie. They are easy to make and melt in your mouth. Check out a popular version of the recipe here.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

I recently found a great recipe for chocolate chip cookies. I omitted the walnuts and substituted 1 cup of Nestle Crunch baking bits and 1 cup of mini semisweet chips for the 2 cups of chocolate chips.

Apps

Waze

Compared to Google Maps and Apple Maps (dreadful!), Waze has been the most consistantly accurate traffic navigator. Visit your phone's store to download.


Pinterest

I was late to the Pinterest party. I just didn't get it...at first. Now I find it a great place to save ideas like recipes, crochet patterns, DIY, home decor and other items. And I can share them with everyone I know (for better or worse).


Gadgets and Gizmos

iPhone

So, I really like my phone. There was a hot minute when my husband and I thought we would switch to the Samsung Note 7. The week that we decided to switch, the exploding battery story came out. Yeah, we'll stick with our non-exploding iPhones (currently on model 7 Plus). It has a good screen size, pretty good camera, all of my apps and music. I would likely be considered an Apple fangirl, but without the blind loyalty. Apple is going to have to step up its game with the next phone. And yes, my phone is pink (ahem, rose gold).

Phone mount

I use my phone (see iPhone and Waze) in the car. I like to be able to see it easily. It's also my jukebox, so it needs to be accessible. Enter the CD slot car mount. My phone fits into the mount nicely and the magnetic attachment method is strong enough to keep it in place. Finally, a use for the CD slot in my car!
